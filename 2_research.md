<section id="working-papers" class="paper-row">
  <p class="paper-status">Working Paper · Draft coming soon</p>
  <h2>Seniority Structure in the Macroeconomy</h2>
  <p class="paper-meta">with <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/adelpheekponon/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Adelphe Ekponon</a> and <a href="https://www.usherbrooke.ca/recherche/fr/specialistes/details/yirlier.hyacinthe.some" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Hyacinthe Somé</a></p>
  <p>Long-term borrowing can dilute incumbent creditors. We combine Capital IQ debt-rank data with a dynamic model to study whether repayment priority reallocates dilution exposure and changes investment, default, and business-cycle propagation.</p>
</section>

<section class="paper-row">
  <p class="paper-status">Working Paper · Draft coming soon</p>
  <h2>Financial Frictions, Intangible Capital, and the Business Cycle</h2>
  <p>I study how real downturns and credit tightening change the composition of firm investment, emphasizing the interaction between intangible exposure and firms’ funding capacity.</p>
</section>
